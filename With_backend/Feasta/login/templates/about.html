About Us

Welcome To FEASTA

Craving some delicious Street food? Maybe you’re in the mood for some exotic dishes? No matter what kind of meal you have in mind, The FEASTA is ready to prepare it for you.

Our diner serves breakfast all day, in addition to wholesome and flavorful dining options for lunch. From burgers to Vadapav, you’ll find all kinds of hearty meals prepared fresh at FEASTA. Sounds delicious, right?

The FEASTA is open daily from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m. We’re here to serve you the best food around.
